Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout when ready
  3. Enter your shipping and payment details
  4. Review your order and confirm
  5. You'll receive an order confirmation email

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • UPI Payments
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (for select locations)

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can:

  1. Log in to your account and check 'My Orders'
  2. Use the tracking link in your confirmation email
  3. Contact our support for help

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please see our Return Policy for full details.
