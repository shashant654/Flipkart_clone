Shipping Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

  • Standard Shipping: 3–5 business days – ₹50 or FREE on orders over ₹500
  • Express Shipping: 1–2 business days – ₹120
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities – ₹200 (Order before 12PM)

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). During peak seasons, processing may take 3–4 business days.

We currently ship to all major cities and towns in India. For remote locations, delivery times may be extended.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package using our website or the courier company's tracking system.

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will contact you to arrange reshipment. Additional shipping charges may apply.

We currently do not offer international shipping. We only deliver within India.
