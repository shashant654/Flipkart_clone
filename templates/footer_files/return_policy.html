Return & Refund Policy

Updated: January 1, 2025

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be in its original condition
  • Item must be in the original packaging
  • Proof of purchase is required

Certain items cannot be returned (e.g., perishable goods, personalized items).

  1. Contact our customer service to initiate a return
  2. Receive a Return Authorization Number
  3. Pack the item securely with all original tags
  4. Include the return form provided by customer service
  5. Ship the package to the provided address

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, defective item, etc.).
